Loop | a billiards game

HOW TO PLAY
  1. 1. Player 1: choose a color.
  2. 2. Take turns trying to pocket your colors.
  3. 3. After that, pocket the black ball to win!
RULES & USAGE
  1. 1. Click and drag the white cue ball to hit it.
  2. 2. Pocket the black too early and you lose.
  3. 3. Same goes for the cue ball.
